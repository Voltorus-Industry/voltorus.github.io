Activity

Voltorus develops electrical equipment designed to explore electricity generation through gravitational energy mechanisms. Our solutions target autonomous, low-carbon, and continuous power production for industrial, private, and off-grid applications.

About Voltorus

Voltorus is dedicated to advancing the scientific understanding of gravity and autonomous energy systems.

The organization focuses on:

  • The study of gravitational machines and gravitational energy
  • Fundamental research into gravitons and quantum gravity
  • Cosmological studies related to the effects of gravity
  • Development of autonomous energy systems

Operating from Paris, Ile-de-France, Voltorus strives to push the boundaries of renewable energy generation by exploring innovative applications of gravitational principles.
